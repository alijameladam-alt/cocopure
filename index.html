Packaging Direction

Our point of difference. Every other brand crowds the label — coconuts, splashes, palms, callouts. We go the opposite way: one flat colour, one bold wordmark, nothing else. Radically minimal packaging that cuts through a noisy shelf and reads instantly.

The wordmark does all the work — heavy rounded type, generous white space, a single ownable colour. And minimal isn't only a look: it lives the “pure” promise (nothing extra, nothing to hide), and it's cheaper to produce — fewer inks, simpler print, less waste — so the savings go into the product, not the packaging.
